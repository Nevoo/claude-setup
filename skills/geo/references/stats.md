| Signal | Correlation with AI Citation | Traditional SEO Value |

| YouTube mentions | ~0.737 (strongest) | Low (not a ranking factor) |
| Reddit mentions | High (exact coefficient not published) | Low |

| LinkedIn presence | Moderate | Low |

| Backlink count | ~0.266 | Very High |

- **Headline finding:** unlinked brand mentions correlate roughly **3x more strongly** with AI visibility than traditional backlinks (Ahrefs, Dec 2025).

- "Reddit" is appended to an estimated **10-15%** of Google searches by users seeking authentic opinions.

## GEO Research Findings (Citability)

Source: Princeton / Georgia Tech / IIT Delhi GEO study (2024), plus Bortolato 2025 analysis.

- GEO-optimized content achieves **30-115% higher** visibility in AI-generated responses (Georgia Tech / Princeton / IIT Delhi 2024).
- Optimal passage length for AI citation: **134-167 words** (Bortolato 2025 analysis of AI Overview passages).
- Definition patterns increase citation rate by **2.1x** (Georgia Tech 2024).
- Adding statistics to passages increases citation by **40%** (Princeton GEO study 2024).
- Adding quotations from authorities increases citation by **up to 115%** in certain categories (IIT Delhi 2024).
- Fluency optimization increases visibility by **~30%** on average across query types.
- Content with source citations is cited **20-25% more often** by Perplexity and ChatGPT search.
